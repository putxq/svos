# QSR Social Pro - Saudi Restaurant Social Media Management

## Problem
Saudi QSR chains lack professional Instagram/Snapchat strategies, struggle to measure social ROI, and have no dedicated resources for consistent content creation and community engagement, resulting in missed customer acquisition and brand visibility opportunities

## Solution
Provide end-to-end social media management service combining content strategy, daily posting, community engagement, and monthly performance analytics specifically tailored for Saudi quick-service restaurants with proven ROI metrics

## Target Customer
Owners and marketing managers of QSR chains (5-50 locations) in Riyadh, Jeddah, and Dammam with annual revenue 2M-20M SAR

## MVP Features
- Monthly social media strategy audit and competitor analysis
- Content calendar creation (30 posts monthly across Instagram/Snapchat)
- Daily community engagement and response management
- Monthly performance dashboard with engagement metrics, reach, and follower growth tracking
- Quarterly strategy optimization based on performance data
