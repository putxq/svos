# RestaurantBoost Riyadh

## Problem
Riyadh restaurants lack affordable, consistent digital marketing; most have no Instagram strategy, waste money on ineffective ads, and miss WhatsApp customer engagement opportunities

## Solution
AI agents create weekly Instagram posts, manage Google Ads campaigns, build WhatsApp broadcast lists, and provide monthly performance reports—all automated and delivered ready-to-use

## Target Customer
Independent restaurants and small chains in Riyadh (50-200 covers/day) with 50k-500k SAR annual revenue

## MVP Features
- Weekly Instagram carousel posts (food photography + captions in Arabic)
- Google Ads campaign setup and weekly optimization (budget $50-200/week)
- WhatsApp broadcast list management and promotional message templates
- Monthly performance dashboard (followers gained, ad spend ROI, engagement rate)
- Customer support via WhatsApp (response within 4 hours)
